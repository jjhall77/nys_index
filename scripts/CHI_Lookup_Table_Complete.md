## Violent Felony Definition: PL 70.02(1)

Only offenses **explicitly enumerated** in PL 70.02(1) are violent felonies:

**Included:**
- Murder 1st & 2nd (125.27, 125.25)
- Manslaughter 1st (125.20)
- Assault 1st & 2nd (120.10, 120.05)
- Gang Assault 1st & 2nd (120.07, 120.06)
- Strangulation 1st & 2nd (121.13, 121.12)
- Stalking 1st (120.60)
- All Robbery degrees (160.15, 160.10, 160.05)
- Burglary 1st & 2nd (140.30, 140.25)

**NOT Included (use non-violent ranges):**
- Manslaughter 2nd (125.15)
- Stalking 2nd (120.55)
- Burglary 3rd (140.20)

## Attempt Adjustment (PL 110.05)

Per Penal Law 110.05, **attempted** crimes are punished one class below the completed offense:

| Completed Class | Completed CHI | Attempt Class | Attempt CHI |
|-----------------|---------------|---------------|-------------|
| A-I (Murder) | 10,950 / 7,300 | B Violent | **5,475** |
| B Violent | 5,475 | C Violent | **3,380** |
| B Non-Violent | 1,825 | C Non-Violent | **1,186** |
| C Violent | 3,380 | D Violent | **1,643** |
| C Non-Violent | 1,186 | D Non-Violent | **456** |
| D Violent | 1,643 | E Violent | **1,004** |
| D Non-Violent | 456 | E Non-Violent | **274** |
| E (any) | 1,004 / 274 | A Misdemeanor | **183** |
| A Misdemeanor | 183 | B Misdemeanor | **45** |

**Application:** Article 125 charges under `offense_description1 = "FELONY ASSAULT"` are automatically treated as attempts.
